Inside Yolo - Company Intranet

Inside Yolo is our company intranet where you can find important information, resources, and stay up-to-date with company news.

How to Access Inside Yolo

1 Open Your Web Browser

Launch your preferred web browser (Chrome recommended).

2 Navigate to the Inside Yolo URL

Enter https://inside.yolotech.com in your browser's address bar.

3 Login with Your Credentials

Use your company email address and the password provided by IT.

Note: If this is your first time logging in, you may be prompted to change your password and set up multi-factor authentication.

Stay updated with company announcements, events, and news.

Access company policies, procedures, and guidelines.

Find contact information for your colleagues across the organization.

Company Calendar

View upcoming company events, holidays, and important dates.

Team Dynamics - Ticket Management System

Team Dynamics is our ticket management system for requesting IT support, facility services, and other internal services.

How to Access Team Dynamics

1 Access via Inside Yolo

Log in to Inside Yolo and click on the "Team Dynamics" link in the top navigation bar.

2 Direct Access

Alternatively, you can access it directly at https://teamdynamics.yolotech.com

Use your company email and password to log in.

How to Submit a Ticket

1 Click "Create New Ticket"

Once logged in, click the "Create New Ticket" button in the top right corner.

2 Select Ticket Type

Choose the appropriate category for your request:

  • IT Support
  • Facilities
  • Human Resources
  • Finance
  • Other Departments
3 Fill Out the Form

Complete the required fields:

  • Subject: Brief description of your issue
  • Description: Detailed explanation of your request
  • Priority: Select based on urgency (Low, Medium, High)
  • Attachments: Add screenshots or other relevant files if needed
4 Submit Your Ticket

Click the "Submit" button to create your ticket.

You'll receive a confirmation email with your ticket number.

Tip: For urgent IT issues, you can also call the IT Help Desk at ext. 1234.

Tracking Your Tickets

1 View "My Tickets"

Click on "My Tickets" in the left navigation panel to see all your active and resolved tickets.

2 Ticket Updates

You'll receive email notifications when there are updates to your tickets.

You can also add comments to your tickets if you need to provide additional information.

Important: Never share your login credentials with others. Each employee should submit their own tickets.

OneDrive - Cloud Storage

OneDrive is our cloud storage solution that allows you to store, access, and share your files securely from any device.

Setting Up OneDrive on Your Computer

1 OneDrive Should Be Pre-installed

OneDrive comes pre-installed on your company laptop.

Look for the OneDrive cloud icon in your system tray (bottom right of your screen).

2 Sign in to OneDrive

If not already signed in:

  1. Click on the OneDrive icon in the system tray
  2. Select "Sign in"
  3. Enter your company email address
  4. Enter your password when prompted
  5. Follow the setup wizard to complete the process
3 Choose Folders to Sync

During setup, you can select which folders to sync to your computer.

By default, all folders will be set to sync. You can change this by:

  1. Right-clicking the OneDrive icon in the system tray
  2. Selecting "Settings"
  3. Going to the "Account" tab
  4. Clicking "Choose folders"

Accessing OneDrive Online

1 Via Web Browser

You can access OneDrive through any web browser:

  1. Go to https://office.com
  2. Sign in with your company email and password
  3. Click on the OneDrive icon in the app launcher
2 Mobile App

You can also access your files on mobile devices:

  1. Download the OneDrive app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
  2. Sign in with your company email and password

Sharing Files and Folders

1 Select the File/Folder

Right-click on the file or folder you want to share.

2 Choose Sharing Options

Select "Share" from the context menu.

Enter the email addresses of the people you want to share with.

Set permissions (View or Edit).

Add a message if needed.

Click "Send" to share.

Important: Be careful when sharing sensitive company information. Only share with those who need access.

Outlook - Email and Calendar

Microsoft Outlook is our email and calendar application for professional communication and scheduling.

Setting Up Outlook

1 Launch Outlook

Outlook should be pre-installed on your company laptop.

Click on the Outlook icon in your taskbar or Start menu.

2 Initial Setup

If this is your first time launching Outlook:

  1. A setup wizard will appear
  2. Enter your company email address
  3. Enter your password when prompted
  4. Outlook will automatically configure your account settings

Key Outlook Features

Email

Send, receive, and manage your emails:

  • Compose: Click "New Email" to create a new message
  • Reply/Forward: Use the Reply, Reply All, or Forward buttons
  • Organize: Create folders to organize your emails
  • Search: Use the search bar to find specific emails
Calendar

Schedule and manage your appointments:

  • View: Click the Calendar icon to switch to calendar view
  • New Event: Double-click on a time slot or click "New Appointment"
  • Meeting: Click "New Meeting" to invite others
  • Sharing: Share your calendar with colleagues for better coordination
Contacts

Manage your professional contacts:

  • Add: Click "New Contact" to add someone to your address book
  • Groups: Create contact groups for easy emailing to teams
  • Company Directory: Access the company-wide address list

Accessing Outlook Web App

1 Via Web Browser

You can access your email from any web browser:

  1. Go to https://outlook.office.com
  2. Sign in with your company email and password
2 Mobile App

Download the Outlook mobile app for access on the go:

  1. Download the Outlook app from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android)
  2. Sign in with your company email and password
Tip: Set up your email signature in Outlook by going to File > Options > Mail > Signatures.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to https://portal.yolotech.com/password
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your company email address
  4. Follow the instructions sent to your email

Alternatively, you can submit a ticket through Team Dynamics for password reset assistance.

How do I connect to the company VPN?

To connect to the company VPN:

  1. Locate the VPN client icon in your system tray
  2. Right-click and select "Connect"
  3. Enter your company email and password
  4. If prompted, complete the multi-factor authentication

For detailed instructions, check the "VPN Setup Guide" in the Document Library on Inside Yolo.

How do I request time off?

To request time off:

  1. Log in to Inside Yolo
  2. Go to "HR Portal" in the top navigation
  3. Click on "Time Off Requests"
  4. Click "New Request"
  5. Fill out the form with your requested dates and reason
  6. Submit for approval

Your manager will receive a notification and will approve or deny your request.

How do I order office supplies?

To order office supplies:

  1. Log in to Team Dynamics
  2. Create a new ticket
  3. Select "Facilities" as the category
  4. Select "Supply Request" as the subcategory
  5. List the items you need in the description
  6. Submit the ticket

The Facilities team will process your request and notify you when your supplies are available for pickup.

How do I book a meeting room?

To book a meeting room:

  1. Open Outlook Calendar
  2. Create a new meeting
  3. Click "Rooms" to see available meeting rooms
  4. Select your preferred room
  5. Add attendees and other meeting details
  6. Send the invitation

You can also view room availability in the "Room Finder" feature in Outlook.
